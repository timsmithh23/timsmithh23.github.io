# Project Pitch

For my Software Engineering capstone, I wanted a project that was challenging and felt like real engineering, not just heavy coding.

LoNSeMonSy stood out because it blends networking, security, DevOps, infrastructure, and systems design. Much of the work focuses on architecture, deployment, and solving operational problems. That mix of skills is what drew me to it as someone that's primarily focusing on networking.
